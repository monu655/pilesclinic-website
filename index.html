Advanced Proctology Care
in Navi Mumbai

Navi Mumbai Piles & Surgical Hospital is a trusted centre for the treatment of piles, fissure and fistula — offering advanced laser procedures with a focus on patient comfort and lasting relief.

Modern Treatment.
Faster Recovery.

We specialize in minimally invasive laser treatments that ensure painless procedures, minimal bleeding and quick recovery. Most treatments are performed as daycare procedures, allowing patients to return home the same day.

Our experienced team focuses on accurate diagnosis and personalized care, ensuring every patient receives the right treatment with complete privacy and comfort.
