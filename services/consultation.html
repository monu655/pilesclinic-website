Proctology Consultation

Confidential one-on-one consultation with our senior proctologists — accurate diagnosis, personalised treatment planning, no judgement.

Why a Proctology Consultation?

Many patients delay seeking help for anorectal conditions due to embarrassment. At our centre, we create a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental environment. Our senior proctologists have extensive experience in diagnosing and managing all anorectal conditions with sensitivity and precision.

What Happens During Consultation?

  • Detailed medical history and thorough symptom discussion
  • Gentle, professional physical and anorectal examination
  • Accurate diagnosis or differential diagnosis
  • Clear explanation of treatment options with pros and cons
  • Personalised treatment plan tailored to your lifestyle and severity
  • Lab tests or imaging ordered if needed
  • Complete privacy and confidentiality at every step

Conditions We Diagnose

  • Piles (hemorrhoids) — all grades
  • Anal fissures — acute and chronic
  • Fistula-in-ano — simple and complex
  • Pilonidal sinus disease
  • Anorectal abscesses
  • Rectal prolapse
  • Anal polyps and papillae
  • Chronic constipation and bowel disorders
  • Unexplained rectal bleeding

Who Should Visit?

  • Anyone with rectal bleeding or blood in stools
  • Persistent anal pain or discomfort
  • Lump or swelling near the anus
  • Discharge of pus or fluid near the anus
  • Itching or burning sensation around the anus
  • Incomplete bowel movements or chronic constipation
  • Previous anorectal surgery with recurring symptoms

Our Consultation Promise

  • Strict patient confidentiality — your privacy is guaranteed
  • Safe, supportive, and non-judgmental environment
  • Thorough and accurate diagnosis
  • Honest assessment of all treatment options
  • Walk-in and appointment slots available
  • Affordable consultation fees
