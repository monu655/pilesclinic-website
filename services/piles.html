Piles (Hemorrhoids) Treatment

Advanced laser-based treatment for all grades of piles with minimal pain, same-day discharge and lasting results.

What Are Piles (Hemorrhoids)?

Piles, medically known as hemorrhoids, are swollen and inflamed veins in the rectum or anus that cause discomfort, bleeding, and pain. They are one of the most common anorectal conditions treated at our hospital.

At Navi Mumbai Piles & Surgical Hospital, we offer advanced, minimally invasive treatments that provide lasting relief with minimal recovery time.

Types of Piles We Treat

  • Grade 1 – Internal piles that bleed but don't prolapse
  • Grade 2 – Prolapse during straining but return on their own
  • Grade 3 – Prolapse during straining and need manual replacement
  • Grade 4 – Permanently prolapsed, cannot be reduced manually
  • External Hemorrhoids – Located under the skin around the anus
  • Thrombosed Hemorrhoids – Emergency clot formation requiring urgent care

Symptoms

  • Bright red bleeding during or after bowel movements
  • Pain, discomfort and itching around the anus
  • Swelling or a lump around the anal region
  • Mucus discharge after passing stool
  • Feeling of incomplete evacuation
  • Soiling or wetness around the anal area

Our Treatment Options

We offer a comprehensive range of evidence-based, patient-specific treatments:

  • Laser Hemorrhoidoplasty (LHP) – Minimally invasive, bloodless, same-day discharge
  • MIPH (Stapler Surgery) – For Grade 3 & 4 piles with rapid recovery
  • Rubber Band Ligation – Office procedure for Grade 1 & 2 piles
  • Sclerotherapy – Injection-based treatment for early-grade piles
  • Ksharsutra Treatment – Traditional Ayurvedic technique with high success
  • Open/Closed Hemorrhoidectomy – For advanced cases requiring surgical removal

Why Choose Laser Treatment?

Laser hemorrhoidoplasty is our preferred technique for eligible patients. It offers significant advantages over conventional surgery:

  • Virtually painless procedure with local or spinal anaesthesia
  • No cuts, no stitches — bloodless procedure
  • Daycare surgery — return home the same day
  • Faster recovery — back to normal activity within 2–3 days
  • Minimal risk of infection and complications
  • High success rate with very low recurrence

Recovery & Post-Surgery Care

Our team provides comprehensive post-operative support including dietary guidance, medication management and follow-up consultations to ensure a complete and smooth recovery.
