Post-Surgery Care

Comprehensive follow-up care, dietary guidance, and recovery support for a smooth and complete healing journey.

Importance of Post-Surgical Care

Recovery after anorectal surgery depends as much on post-operative care as the procedure itself. Proper aftercare reduces complications, speeds up healing, prevents recurrence, and ensures the best possible long-term outcome. Our team provides dedicated follow-up support throughout your entire recovery journey.

What Our Post-Surgery Care Includes

  • Detailed discharge instructions for home care
  • Prescribed medications: pain relief, anti-inflammatories, stool softeners
  • Dietary plan: high-fibre foods, hydration guidance, foods to avoid
  • Wound care instructions: sitz baths, hygiene protocols
  • Activity restrictions and gradual return to normal life
  • Scheduled follow-up visits at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months
  • 24/7 WhatsApp support for post-operative concerns

Dietary Guidelines After Surgery

  • Increase fibre: fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes
  • Drink at least 8–10 glasses of water daily
  • Avoid spicy, oily, and processed foods for 2–3 weeks
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking — both impair healing
  • Small, frequent meals are better than large ones
  • Probiotics may be recommended to restore gut health

Common Post-Op Concerns We Address

  • Pain management strategies and medication guidance
  • Managing initial constipation after surgery
  • What to expect with wound healing and discharge
  • Signs of complications and when to call us
  • Gradual resumption of physical activity and exercise
  • Patient reassurance and psychological support

Long-Term Recurrence Prevention

  • Sustained high-fibre diet and lifestyle modifications
  • Regular physical activity to improve bowel function
  • Avoiding prolonged sitting or straining
  • Annual check-up with our proctology team
  • Weight management if indicated
  • Management of related conditions like IBS or chronic constipation
