About Dr. Deepak Bhangale

Dr. Dipak Bhangale is a highly trained specialist in Medical Gastroenterology, Hepatobiliary Medicine, Pancreatic Disorders, and Transplant Medicine. He is recognised as one of the best gastroenterologists in Navi Mumbai, combining extensive international training with exceptional clinical expertise.

Dr. Bhangale completed a Fellowship in Advanced Endoscopy at PGI Punjab, a Fellowship in Endoscopic Ultrasound at Midas Hospital, Nagpur, and an advanced Fellowship in Endoscopic Submucosal Dissection and Magnifying Endoscopy at NTT Medical Center, Tokyo, Japan — reflecting his commitment to world-class standards of care.

He provides comprehensive care for a wide range of gastrointestinal and liver conditions at GI Care Navi Mumbai, with clinics in both Kharghar and Vashi. He is available for both in-person and emergency consultations, ensuring patients always have access to expert GI care when they need it most.
